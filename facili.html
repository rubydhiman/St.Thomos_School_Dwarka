FACILITIES

FACILITIES

A vast expanse of 10 acres of lush green lawns, well-guarded building and child-friendly interior spaces offers a perfect environment for an aesthetically commissioned school building. The open lawns are marked with a colourful bonanza of fresh floral bounty.



  • An awe-inspiring infrastructure comprising of well-ventilated classrooms, science laboratories and libraries aid the process of learning. We have state-of-art dance studio, an AV room, Conference room, and an art gallery to showcase the creative flair of the students. Smartboards by TATA Edge bring technology to the lessons taught.
  • Campus has large open spaces as well as appropriately planned buildings for Pre-primary/ Primary/ Senior wings providing ample space for indoor and outdoor activities.
  • Silent generators are installed to ensure a constant, effective power supply.
  • State of art indoor and outdoor sports facilities.
  • Secured and CCTV-monitored campus.
  • Fire alarms and fire fighting systems add to the security further.

LIBRARY

The School has a well-stocked library to cater the needs of students as well as its staff members. It has more than 20,000 titles, which caters to its staff and students. It boasts of a rich treasure trove of encyclopaedias, illustrated dictionaries, and popular science books along with series such as Tell Me Why and the Book of Knowledge. It is well-stocked with classic and contemporary works of literature in both Hindi and English languages. Furthermore, there is a worthy collection of latest versions in career counselling, personality development, etc. The library also subscribes to a huge number of periodicals and magazines to help students as well as the teachers stay up-to-date with events from around the world as well as discover and reconnoitre different career avenues. It provides quiet and tranquil environs where children can come to satiate their quest for knowledge.

CHAPEL

Chapel is a powerful opportunity for our students to learn more about the Lord, to grow in knowledge of His character, to deepen their relationships with the Almighty, and to support each other through prayer as well as to share in worship. St. Thomas’ School, Dwarka aims to provide a variety of spiritual experiences so that students develop life-long spiritual disciplines that lead to a successful life. Spiritual training partnered with Chapel makes a powerful team in reaching this goal.

Art & Craft

Our school has beautifully planned Art Rooms that inspires children to unleash their creativity and imagination. We have different Art Rooms for different departments keeping in view the different age groups. Art Rooms are equipped with different facilities. All students learn the basics of sketching. Lessons in still life, landscape, and portrait are taught. Techniques with pencil, markers, charcoal, pastels, and acrylic and water colours are demonstrated. Personal creativity is stressed.

Infirmary

We have a well-resourced and well-equipped, air conditioned medical facility room with a trained& well qualified staff-nurse. The medical centre has 7 beds and is equipped with Nebulizer, BP instrument, emergency medicines and other latest equipment. A complete medical and dental check-up is conducted bi-annually for the students. The students’ health and well-being are the school’s priority.

Band

To keep the students grounded to their roots and uphold the strong cultural tradition, regular and well organized cultural activities form an integral part of the schooling system. It inculcates leadership and confidence in each and every student. Our Band room is well equipped with various String, Brass, Woodwind and Percussion music instruments like Keyboard, Guitar, Drums and many other instruments like Kongo, Harmonium, Dholki, etc. The students are encouraged to learn to play different instruments. Students actively take part in different types of competitions at school and inter-school level.

Safety

Schools are changing. Responsibilities for safety and health management are being refocused. Safety is imperative and needs to be advanced resourcefully and should not be seen as merely another legal liability or administrative job. A strategic approach to managing risk should be seen as an enabler, not just to avert accidents and work related health problems to both staff and pupils but to build a culture of sensible risk management, linked to a curriculum where teaching young people can develop their capability to assess and manage risk. Risk is part of life but accidents do not need to be, so while schools need to make sure staff, pupils and visitors are safe, they also need to make sure that, as the citizens of tomorrow, students are helped to become risk aware without becoming needlessly risk averse. In this sense ‘teaching safely’ and ‘teaching safety’ need to go hand in hand. St. Thomas’ School Dwarka conducts mock drills and teaches Disaster Management techniques through workshops as well as the curriculum, to prepare its staff and students to face all sorts of natural and man-made disasters. Controlled monitoring system is ensured through more than 300 CCTV cameras installed across the campus.

Transport

The school runs a fleet of buses catering to specified routes in Delhi only. Our buses are speed governed and have conductors and female teachers/ attendants to ensure the safety of the on-board students.
