WELCOME TO ST. THOMAS' SCHOOL

...

HITTING THE BULL's EYE
EXCELLENCE IN MOTION

...

St. Thomas' School, founded in 1930 by Miss Helen Jerwood as a Diocesan School, is permanently recognised and prepares students for the All India Senior School Certificate Examination. St. Thomas' School, Dwarka is an extension of the parent school, and established in the April, 2006. We are a progressive school and seek to provide the most effective education to every student who enters our doors in search of learning. We believe in developing a caring community, exercising concern and respect for the welfare of others and emphasizing the over-riding importance of good Human relations, based upon sensitivity, tolerance and good-will.

ST. THOMAS' AT A GLANCE

We provide opportunities to succeed

We encourage students to appreciate the virtues of collaboration Foster habits of responsibility and self-discipline Promote initiative, endeavour and the exercise of individual judgement.
We project the school as the servant of the community and to accept its appropriate share of responsibility for equipping each child to finalise his/her own niche in his/her occupation and in society. We inculcate values that serve as guiding principles for better citizens of tomorrow.
